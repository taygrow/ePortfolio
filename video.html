Video Production

iMovies Was Used to Create this Educational Video

For this video presentation, I chose to present on the city of Braunschweig, Germany. I had the opportunity to spend the semester there this summer, so it holds a special place in my heart. For my images, I chose to use a title card and a references image. Additionally, I picked out ten images that wholeheartedly represented Braunschweig and incorporated them into the presentation. I crafted a short narration for each of the images and recorded it in VoiceMemos on my iPhone. Utilizing iMovie, I brought all twelve images into a new project and overlayed my voice recording. Then, I used a soundtrack that was provided for free via iMovies, and brought that into the project. I set the voiceover to the foreground, and added in transitions throughout all of the images. Additionally, I utilized the Ken Burns effect on many of my images to really guide the viewer through the presentation.
